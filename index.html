EchoField respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how EchoField handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

EchoField is designed to help you inspect and understand your local network environment. Depending on how you use the app, EchoField may access or display:

  • Information about your current local network connection
  • Nearby or connected devices detected on your local network
  • Device names, hostnames, IP addresses, and related diagnostic details when available
  • Wi-Fi and connection-related technical information made available by iOS

2. How This Information Is Used

The information accessed by EchoField is used only to provide the app’s core functionality, including:

  • Discovering devices on your local network
  • Displaying device and network details
  • Running local network diagnostics
  • Helping you troubleshoot connectivity issues

3. Local Network Permission

EchoField requests access to your local network so it can discover devices and perform network diagnostics within your own LAN environment. Without this permission, some features of the app may not function.

4. Location Permission

On iOS, certain Wi-Fi and network-related information may require location permission. If EchoField requests location access, it is used only to enable those system-provided network features. EchoField is not designed to track your precise location for advertising or profiling purposes.

5. Data Storage

EchoField is intended to process network information on your device. Unless otherwise stated in the app, EchoField does not require you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information for remote storage.

6. Data Sharing

EchoField does not sell your personal information. EchoField does not share your local network data with third parties except where necessary to comply with applicable law, legal process, or governmental request.

7. Third-Party Services

If the app later includes optional third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, or support tools, this Privacy Policy should be updated to reflect those services and their data practices.

8. Data Retention

Any information displayed by EchoField is generally used for in-app functionality. If the app stores preferences or recent diagnostic results locally on your device, that information remains under your control and may be removed by deleting the app.

9. Children’s Privacy

EchoField is not directed specifically to children under 13, and the app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to design EchoField in a privacy-conscious manner. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
